# Estilos

## Boas Práticas

- **bold text**
- *italic text*
- _underline text_
- ~~strikethrough text~~
- ==mark==
- ***bold and italic text***
- ***_bold and italic and underline text_***
- ***_==bold and italic and underline and mark text==_***
- *_italic and underline text_*
- ~sub~script
- ^super^script
- [[in box]]
- <kbd>Keyboard by HTML</kbd>
- `code`
- ``Use `code` Escaping Backticks``
- color **red**{style="color:red"} Markdown
- color <font color=red>red</font> HTML

## Não Recomendado

- __bold text__
- ___bold and underline text___
